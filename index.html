About Us

Welcome to Shenzhen Lumière Designs, a leading Chinese company that specializes in creating exquisite lighting designs enhanced with precious stones (pierres précieuses). Our artisans combine cutting-edge technology with traditional craftsmanship to deliver unique, high-quality lighting fixtures.

Each of our products is a masterpiece, with carefully selected precious stones embedded into every design to bring an element of elegance and sophistication. Whether it's a chandelier, table lamp, or pendant light, every piece tells a story of luxury, creativity, and craftsmanship.

Founded in 2010 in Shenzhen, our team of designers and artisans work tirelessly to blend the beauty of light with the splendor of precious stones, creating pieces that shine with grace and brilliance.
